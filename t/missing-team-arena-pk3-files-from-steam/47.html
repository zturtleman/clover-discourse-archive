coltongit 2016-06-30 16:21:15 UTC #2

pak4.pk3 doesn’t exist, but pak0-3 does. The pak4 was an error in the readme, but you’ve come to the right place if you need a better engine for your Steam Q3A. This one’s got splitscreen support, native 16:9 resolution UI, & a crap ton of bug fixes against the one provided by Steam. And it’s still usable with your Steam copy, all the way down to scorekeeping, etc. The only thing that it doesn’t have is compatibility with older Q3 mods, demo replays, & even the master server (!) like the original does, mainly due to unification of the ui.qvm with cgame.qvm. And Spearmint’s open source, too.

zturtleman 2016-06-30 18:14:55 UTC #5

A zip of the official Q3 / Team Arena patch pk3s is available at http://ioquake3.org/extras/patch-data/. I’ll link to that in the read me in the next release and remove the reference to missionpack/pak4.pk3.

Updated read me text for the next release:

To play Spearmint Quake 3, copy Quake 3’s baseq3/pak[0-8].pk3 into the local baseq3 directory. To play the Team Arena expansion, copy Quake 3 Team Arena’s missionpack/pak[0-3].pk3 into the local missionpack directory.

If you don’t own Quake 3 or Team Arena, they can be purchased on Steam. http://store.steampowered.com/sub/433/
Team Arena is not required to play Quake 3.

If you’re missing some of the patch pk3s (pak1 or higher), they are available at http://ioquake3.org/extras/patch-data/
